.:Backstory:.
Baurn the legendary stick-monger went into his back yard to get 50 sticks, so he could built a great bow for his son. Reymie.
He quickly found himself needing some help finding sticks, so he's asking you to help him.





.:Controls:.

Just use the arrow keys to move Baurn.
Also, use 'q' key to quit when you collect all 50 sticks.
Have fun!
